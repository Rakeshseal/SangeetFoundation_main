Happiness Through Music And The Arts

Sangeet Foundation has been granted charity status by Charity Commission and is registered as Sangeet Global Ltd. Charity Reg Number: 1195762 Company Reg No. 12966630. At Happiness Through Music and the Arts, we believe in the transformative power of creativity to bring joy, healing, and inspiration to people's lives. Our mission is to promote happiness and well-being through the exploration and appreciation of music and various art forms.

Explore the Melodies of Joy:


Immerse yourself in the captivating world of music. Discover articles, playlists, and insights that explore the profound impact of melodies on our emotions. From classical compositions that stir the soul to contemporary tunes that make your heart dance, we delve into how music has the remarkable ability to elevate moods and ignite feelings of happiness.

Vision

We aim to be a companion to all who struggle with mental health, Provide the networking platform using Music and The Arts so that no one should struggle alone with their mental health.

Mission

Create awareness and understanding about mental health, Provide sign posts for people seeking help, create a network to help the South Asian Community in the UK and provide music and arts based intervention.

Values

Better together - Making a difference - Relentless - Always available
